Our Story

The Soul of Amritsar
in the Heart of Lahore

Rooted in the legendary food streets of Amritsar, our recipes have crossed borders and generations to land in the heart of Johar Town, Lahore. Every dish we prepare carries the memory of Laxmi Chowk — where slow cooking, pure ghee, and whole spices created food that became the pride of Punjab.

We cook the way it has always been done — with time, with patience, and with love. No shortcuts, no compromises. Just the real, honest taste of Amritsari cuisine on every plate.
